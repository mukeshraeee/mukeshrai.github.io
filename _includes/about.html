About me

I hold a MasterS in Environmental Science [Tribhuvan University, Nepal] and M.S by research in Glaciology [Kathmandu University, Nepal]. Currently, I am working as PhD fellow at UCAS, China. My specialization is atmospheric science. Part of my PhD works includes the aerosol simulation using WRF-Chem model and multi-sensors satellite. I have experience with the high performance computing (HPC) and Linux system. I have a keen interest in aerosol transport mechanism, synergy on aerosol-boundary layer interaction, and radiative forcing.
